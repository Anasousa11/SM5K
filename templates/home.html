ABOUT US

My Story

Founded by Paul Ola, SinMancha grew from a personal journey of redemption into a tight-knit running community. Paul combines hard-earned street experience with professional training to guide people toward transformation.

Our Mission

We believe in second chances, resilience, and using fitness as a tool for change. SinMancha provides structured training, supportive community, and opportunities for growth—both physical and personal.
